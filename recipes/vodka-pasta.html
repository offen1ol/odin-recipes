Vodka Pasta

Description

Based off of YouTuber's Evan and Katelyn's Vodka Sauce Recipe

Super simple and easy to make when you are craving pasta. I pair it with steamed broccoli and add mushrooms to the sauce.

They have a recipe for their Magic Broccoli which is good too but sometimes I'm too lazy to make it after making this dish.

Ingredients

~4 serving size

    Main

  • 1 box of pasta (I get angel hair)

    • The original recipe they use penne

  • Steamed broccoli

    • Feel free to add whatever protein or veggies you like. I think spinach would also pair nicely in the sauce.

    Sauce

  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 2tbsp butter
  • 1 medium white onion
  • 1 pack of sliced white mushrooms
  • 3tsp minced garlic
  • 3/4-1cup white wine, red wine, or vodka (no sweet wine)

    • I use the Barefoot Pinot Grigio

  • 14/15oz canned tomato puree
  • 2/3 heavy cream
  • red pepper flakes (to taste)
  • 1/4tsp kosher salt
  • black pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4-1/2cup shredded parmesan

Steps

  1. Chop up onions and mushrooms. I'm too lazy to dice so I just roughly chop them up
  2. Heat butter and oil in a medium or large pot on medium-high heat
  3. Add in the onions and mushrooms until onions are translucent
  4. Add in the garlic and cook until veggies are soft
  5. Pour in the chosen alcohol and cook until the liquid is reduced by half
  6. You can start boiling water to cook the pasta in a separate pot now and follow the box instructions on how long to cook the noodles
  7. Add tomato puree and stir constantly for a few minutes
  8. Reduce heat to low and add the heavy cream, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper, and parmesan
  9. Cook until the parmesan is melted and evenly incorporated
  10. Add a little olive oil to the strained pasta to avoid sticking
  11. steam the broccoli, and just add on top of the pasta and sauce with more parmesan and red pepper flakes
