Apple Galette

Description

This is so quick and easy to make if you want a light dessert. Although my family would eat it all in one sitting...

My mom was obsessed with this when I used to live with her. She would ask me to make this for her constantly and would buy the supplies preemptively... so I know it's good!

It's basically like a simplistic apple pie if you aren't into the heavy crust or the thick apple pie filling of a normal pie.

✨The thin crust pizza version of a pie✨

Ingredients

Feel free to skip optional

  • 2 apples (I used green apples so they would be as mushy after baking)
  • 1 premade pie dough sheet (I used Pillsbury)
  • 1/4tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4tsp ginger
  • 1/4tsp allspice
  • 1/2cup sugar
  • 1tbsp flour
  • egg wash (1 egg needed)
  • butter (to your judgement)
  • 2 1/2tbsp lemon juice

Steps

Feel free to skip optional

  1. preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. slice apples, thinner makes it easier to fold pastry later
  3. optional: put the sliced apples a bowl of water and mix in the lemon juice, this prevents apples from turning brown before baking
  4. optional: microwave apples in the lemon water for 5 minutes to soften the slices. mix halfway through to evenly soften
  5. unroll the sheet of pie dough on a cookie sheet pan to soften at room temperature
  6. optional: drain the apple slices from the water if you followed earlier optional steps
  7. in a separate large bowl combine all spices and sugar together
  8. add in the apple slices into the bowl of spices and sugar and mix well until all apples are well coates
  9. spread the apple slices in your preferred pattern in a circle making sure to keep an inch margin of pie dough uncovered
  10. fold the pie dough around the apple mixture, I usually pleat the dough when folding to make it neat
  11. get the egg wash (1 egg and some water and mix well together) and brush it on the folded pie crust until well covered, makes the dough golden
  12. get a little bit of butter and put it on the top of the exposed apple mixture. can have multiple little pads of butter to evenly melt
  13. add a pinch of sugar around the pie crust
  14. bake for about 25 minutes
  15. let cool for 10 minutes
  16. Enjoy! Can add vanilla ice cream too!
