Honey Sriracha Salmon Rice Bowl

A quick meal to make that is a healthy and balanced dish. The simple dish of just salmon fillet, broccoli florets, and jasmine rice is immediately elevated by the accompanied sauce.

The honey sriracha sauce is also minimal in the ingredients required and is easily versatile to match one's partiality to strong or subdued spice.

Can easily be a cheap meal using frozen salmon and broccoli (which is what I usually do...) so you can have it whenever you want without worrying about expiratory dates.

Ingredients

2 serving size

    Main

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • ~6oz broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup of jasmine white rice

    • Sauce

  • 4tbsp honey
  • 4tbsp sriracha
  • 1tsp sesame seeds
  • 1tsp sesame oil
  • 1tsp rice vinegar

Steps

  1. Bake salmon for appropriate amount
  2. Add 3/4 cup water to rice
  3. Cook rice
  4. Steam broccoli
  5. combine all sauce ingredients in a small cup with a lid to seal for later

    6. this will made 2 servings and can save the second portion for the next day and cook salmon on as needed
