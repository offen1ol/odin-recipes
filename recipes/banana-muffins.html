Banana Muffins

Description

This is Katelyn's award-winning banana muffins recipe from Evan and Katelyn on YouTube.

These are super easy to and quick to make and you don't need that many ingredients to make them from scratch.

You can customize them too. I prefer a little crunch so I added walnuts to mine. But chocolate chips would work well too!

For toppings, they normally melt butter and sprinkle cinnamon sugar on top. But I came up with an alternative...

If you have ever been to Texas Roadhouse, you know about their whipped cinnamon honey butter. I found some at my local Sam's Club that you can buy. Another idea is to add some Nutella on top too.

This makes about 10 regular sized muffins.

Ingredients

Original Recipe

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp of nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup of softened butter
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar - packed in
  • 1 egg
  • 2.5 - 3 mashed very ripe bananas - if less ripe, add more sugar
  • 3/4 tsp of vanilla

Add-Ins Ideas

  • chopped walnuts
  • semi-sweet chocolate chips

Toppings Ideas

  • Melted butter and cinnamon sugar
  • Whipped Cinnamon Honey Butter
  • Nutella

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 325deg F
  2. mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl: flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and any add-ins of your choosing. this helps with them not sinking during baking.
  3. mash up bananas and set off to the side
  4. cream butter and brown sugar together in a medium bowl
  5. combine the mashed up bananas, egg, and vanilla with the butter and sugar until evenly mixed
  7. add in the wet mix to the dry mix until just combined. add in the wet mix in little batches to avoid over mixing the batter.
  8. prep your baking tin with liners and scoop the mix into them
  9. bake for 17-22 minutes or until clean toothpick. There will still be a little wet but can also try to tap the top to see how firm it is.
  10. let cool for a couple minutes

Optional Toppings

For melted butter and cinnamon sugar

  • poke holes in muffins with a fork
  • pour melted butter over muffins
  • sprinkle the sugar on top
