Tea Latte

Description

Top Secret Insider Knowledge (super simple easy) recipe for a tea latte lol

It's probably common knowledge on how to make a tea latte but just for future reference, Ta-Da!

Can use this to apply to any tea latte. I make mine super simple honestly...

Ingredients

  • tea of choice

    • 1 bag for 16/20oz or 2 bags for 24oz

    They use Fraser Tea (as of 2023) in the past they used Harney & Sons brand

  • milk of choice

    • I prefer Chobani Extra Creamy Oat Milk. It doesn't have that strong oat or vanilla flavor to it.*

    They use the Califia Oat Milk. I think it's too vanilla-y but my co-workers really like this brand.

    *I am a changed person. I, now, enjoy the Califia brand. Even the store-bought creamy oat milk is just as good as the barista blend. They have the perfect creamy texture.

  • optional: vanilla syrup

    • They use the Monin brand of Vanilla

    Crucial when using 2% regular milk or the tea of choice might curdle the milk

    I don't personally use this, it overpowers the tea imo

    3 pumps for 16oz, 4 pumps for 20oz, 5 pumps for 24oz

  • optional: ice
  • optional: sweet foam

    • They use Rich's On Top Brand of Sweet Foam

Steps

Iced Latte

  1. in a cup, steep tea in hot water to your preferred strength. I steep for a long time.

    2. 3oz for 16oz, 4oz for 20oz, 5oz 24oz

  2. fill separate cup with ice
  3. cover ice in milk
  4. optional: add in vanilla syrup
  5. add in steeped tea to milk and stir well
  6. optional: Bonus! add sweet foam to it!

Hot Latte

requires a milk steamer which I don't have... which is why I make it iced at home ðŸ˜…

  1. Place tea bags into cup
  2. optional: Add vanilla syrup
  3. Steam milk until 140 deg F

    4. 120 for Not Hot, 160 for Extra Hot

  4. Stir and let steep before drinking
  5. optional: Sweet Foam is also great in hot drinks! Make sure to foam milk well or it'll sink
