Vermicelli Stir Fry

Description

Gotta love throwing a bunch of ingredients in a pot with a good sauce and hoping it works out...

If you love veggies than this is a recipe for you. Super customizable so just pick ingredients you like and think would work in a stir fry. You really can't go wrong here.

Based off of this recipe that I loosely followed along to

Mentally prepare for a lot of choppping...

Ingredients

~4 or 5 serving size

    Main

  • 8oz vermicelli (dried mung bean) noodles
  • optional: 5oz protein of choice
  • 1 medium/small white onion
  • 1 large/medium carrot
  • 1 pack of shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 baby bok choy
  • 1 bundle of green onions/scallions
  • +2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • +1tsp of minced ginger

    • Sauce

  • 2tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 3/4cup stock or water

    • if you are cooking protein, double this recipe and add in chili oil to your spice preference

    Optional Toppings

  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  • chili oil
  • green onions/scallions

Steps

  1. combine all sauce ingredients together in a small bowl
  2. optional: cut protein of choice in small pieces
  3. optional: can add 1/3 of sauce and a 1tsp of chili oil to protein to marinade preferably for about 2 hours or more
  4. Julienne cut all vegetables and set aside
  5. Soak noodles in warm water for 15 minutes
  6. After 15 minutes, drain the noodles and combine vegetable oil and soy sauce (I did 1.5tbsp of each) and evenly coat the noodles to prevent sticking
  7. Heat a large pot to medium/high heat until water evaporates on contact
  8. add 1/2 of thee vegetable oil, 1/2 of the white part of scallions, and 1/2 of the minced garlic until fragrant
  9. optional: cook protein until browned then remove from pot and set aside
  10. heat the pot back up to a medium/high heat and add in the other halves of vegetable oil, white scallions, and garlic
  11. add in onions and carrots until onions are soft/caramelized
  12. add in the mushrooms and cook until soft
  13. add in bok choy, half of the sauce, chosen protein, and minced ginger and combine in the pot
  14. add noodles and the rest of the sauce to the pot until all of the sauce is soaked into the noodles evenly
  15. optional: add on optional toppings to preference
