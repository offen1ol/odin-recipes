Vermicelli Stir Fry

Description

Gotta love throwing a bunch of ingredients in a pot with a good sauce and hoping it works out...

If you love veggies than this is a recipe for you. Super customizable so just pick ingredients you like and think would work in a stir fry. You really can't go wrong here.

Mentally prepare for a lot of choppping...

Ingredients

~4 or 5 serving size

    Main

  • 8oz vermicelli (dried mung bean) noodles
  • optional: 5oz protein of choice
  • 1 medium/small white onion
  • 1 large/medium carrot
  • 1 pack of shiitake mushrooms
  • 3 baby bok choy
  • 1 bundle of green onions/scallions
  • +2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • +1tsp of minced garlic

    • Sauce

  • 2tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tsp sugar
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 3/4cup stock or water

    • Optional Toppings

  • sesame oil
  • sesame seeds
  • chili oil
  • green onions/scallions

Steps

  1. combine all sauce ingredients together in a small bowl
  2. Julienne cut all vegetables and set aside
  3. optional: cut protein of choice in small pieces
  4. optional: can add 1/3 of sauce to protein to marinade shortly
  5. Soak noodles in warm water for 15 minutes
  6. After 15 minutes, drain the noodles and combine vegetable oil and soy sauce (I did 1.5 of each) and evenly coat the noodles to prevent sticking
  7. Heat a large pot to medium/high heat until water evaporates on contact
  8. add vegetable oil, white part of scallions, and minced garlic until fragrant
  9. optional: cook protein until browned
  10. add in onions, carrots, and mushrooms until onions are soft/caramelized
  11. add in bok choy, half of the sauce, and minced garlic and combine in the pot
  12. add noodles and the rest of the sauce to the pot until all of the sauce is soaked into the noodles evenly
  13. optional: add on optional toppings to preference
